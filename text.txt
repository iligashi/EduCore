1. Workflow Automation

Inspired by:

Jira
Notion
Canvas
Smart Ideas
Automatic Assignment Pipeline

When instructor creates assignment:

notification sent automatically
deadline added to calendar
student dashboard updated

Automatic Grade Rules

Example:

assignment submitted late

Every week:

instructors receive course analytics
admins receive attendance summaries
students receive performance summaries

Huge “real-world system” feeling.
